    About

    So, who is Charlie? Great question.

    I am a Software Engineer based in Los Angeles looking for an opportunity to learn and improve my skills as a developer.

    What started as an interesting activity to do after work became a passion. I was intent on teaching myself how to code, but soon understood that if this passion were to become a reality I would need professional guidance. I decided to take the leap.

    I attended App Academy, a rigorous full stack software engineering bootcamp where I learned and solidified the fundamentals of full stack development, graduating in March 2022.

    My technical skills are in languages and frameworks such as React, Redux, JavaScript Postgresql, Ruby on Rails, Ruby, HTML, CSS, MongoDB and Mongoose. With my attention to detail and desire for smooth user experience I know I'll be able to learn fast and meet any challenge placed in front of me.
