    About

    So, who is Charlie? Great question.

    I grew up in Vermont and eventually made my way to New York City where I started a career in sales. I made countless cold calls and eventually realized I wanted something different.

    What started as an interesting activity to do after work became a passion. I was intent on teaching myself how to code, but soon understood that if this passion were to become a reality I would need professional guidance. I decided to take the leap.

    From December to March of 2019 I attended General Assembly's Web Development Immersive program, learning the fundamentals of full stack development.

    For the past year and a half I have had the ability to expand my knowledge and talents in software engineering, working on projects and new technologies to keep up with the fast moving times, all while maintaining a full time position in sales. I am truly excited for what lies in the future and am more than ready to fulfill my dream of becoming a software engineer.
